Our Pastor
R.B. Thieme III

Pastor Robert B. Thieme III, a native Texan, became pastor of Berachah Church in 2004 following his father Colonel Robert B. Thieme Jr., who built a worldwide ministry while serving as pastor for 53 years. Prior to becoming a pastor, R. B. Thieme III had a distinguished career in the U. S. Army serving as an Infantry officer, Airborne Ranger, and a tactical and strategical intelligence officer. He left the army as a major and enrolled in Western Seminary in Portland, Oregon. He graduated Magna Cum Laude in 1987 with Masters degrees of Divinity and Theology. Upon completion of seminary in 1988, he joined R. B. Thieme Jr., Bible Ministries as the editor-in-chief. He continues to serve as president of the Ministries as well as pastor of Berachah Church.

What to Expect

We welcome all visitors! We work hard to provide a friendly environment for all, as well as sound biblical materials to help you on your Christian walk, whether you are new to Christianity or have been a Christian for some time.


The class starts promptly at 9:00am on Sundays. Communion, which all are welcome to participate in, is held on the first Sunday of each month at the beginning of the class. We are currently following the videos of pastor R.B. Thieme III, the leader of Berachah Church, located in Texas.


Please reach out to us with any questions! You can contact us by any of the means on this page.

Membership & Giving

Church membership is not required for attendance or communion. If you are interested in becoming a member, you may contact us by the means on this site, found on this page. The Bible Doctrine Church of West Michigan never solicits membership or money.
